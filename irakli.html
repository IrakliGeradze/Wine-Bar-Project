Irakli Geradze
Founder & CEO

Irakli Geradze is the visionary Founder and CEO of Wine Bar. With a passion for viticulture that spans decades, he established this establishment to bring the finest wines from around the world to our local community. His leadership and dedication to quality ensure that every guest experiences the true essence of hospitality. Irakli believes that wine is not just a drink, but a story in every bottle, and he is dedicated to sharing those stories with you.
